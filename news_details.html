Certificate in Understanding Law and Interpretation of Statutes

Understanding Law and interpretation of status is an extensive program whose focus is on basis principles of law, the process of enacting a law, and the interpretation.The course is primarily designed for persons that are : parliamentarians, local government practioners,policy makers, councillors, and any other persions who wisht to understand law and interpretation of status.

The course, taught by eminent scholars and practitioners, is composed of several extensive moduels that are designed to bring into interpretation of status.Thus, undertaking this course is a hug benefit to any candidate as it broadens a student understanding of law as well as equipping them with necessary interpretational legal skills.

This course is composed of numerous modeules which include:

  • Nature and classification of status
  • Law making process
  • Role of Parliamentary and Council Committees
  • Role of the Constitutional Court in parliamentary oversight
  • Judiciary as interpreters of the law
  • Theories and rules of interpretation
  • Interpretational tools

    • DURATION

    The program is for six months, twice a week from 17:30 - 19:30. The devlivery of the program will be block release with assessments done

    QUALIFICATIONS

    A candidate must posses a full grade twelve certificate or equivalent, and must also be a person who is either a parliamentarian, invlolved in local authority administration, policy making, councilior, or one whose work is connected to drafting of legislation.
