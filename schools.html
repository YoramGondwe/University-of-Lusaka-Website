SCHOOL OF HEALTH SCIENCES


The School of Health Sciences offers a Bachelor of Science in Public Health which is a market - and needs - driven program with the objective of responding to the demand of stakeholders. The programme focuses on the 'whole' in terms of public health competencies and pedagogy to satisfy the urgent needs for multifaceted public health training in Zambia and beyond. It is a combination of sciences and skills directed at the maintenance and improvement of the health of people. ....

SCHOOL OF LAW


The School of Law is one of the leading and innovative Law schools in Zambia and its major aims are to provide quality education tailor made to suit the demands of the market and to give its students an education that prepares them for successful careers in legal practice and work in various sectors of society at the national, regional and international level.
