SCHOOL OF BUSINESS & MANAGEMENT

UNDERGRADUATE

The School of Business and Management was the first discipline when the University opened its doors. It was established with the mission to be a center of excellence in education, training, research and consultancy services. Since then, the school has developed to become the biggest school at the university and the programmes are designed to enable the graduates to acquire knowledge, skills and competences that are acceptable in both the private and public sector nationally, regionally and beyond. Since commencing, the school has commanded a prestigious ranking in the nation due to its composition of high-calibre academic staff drawn from the private and public sectors.In response to current needs of the environment, the vision of the School of Business is to be the first choice for students seeking a business education and the first choice for employers seeking business graduates. Further, the school has a vision to become a leading research and consultancy centre.
